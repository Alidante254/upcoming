About Upcoming

Upcoming is an innovative app designed to empower and support upcoming artists in their musical journey. Our platform provides a comprehensive suite of tools and features to help artists record, improve, and share their music with the world.

Features

  • Record your songs using our state-of-the-art recording software. Our intuitive interface allows you to easily capture your musical ideas and bring them to life.
  • Get valuable feedback from a vibrant community of fellow upcoming artists. Share your music, receive constructive criticism, and grow together.
  • Explore a vast collection of song lyrics to find inspiration or contribute your own. Our lyrics database is constantly expanding, fueling creativity and collaboration.
  • Discover new talents by listening to songs posted by other upcoming artists. Support each other, collaborate, and create meaningful connections.
  • Engage in discussions and forums dedicated to various aspects of music production, songwriting, and performance. Learn from experienced artists and exchange insights.
  • Personalize your profile and showcase your unique style. Customize your artist page with images, bio, and links to your social media platforms.

Why Use Upcoming?

Upcoming is the ultimate destination for aspiring artists to unleash their creativity, develop their skills, and connect with a supportive community. Here's why you should choose Upcoming:

  • Inspiration: Surround yourself with talented artists, explore diverse music genres, and find inspiration to fuel your own artistic journey.
  • Growth: Receive valuable feedback and learn from fellow artists. Our platform is designed to foster growth and help you refine your craft.
  • Networking: Connect with like-minded individuals, collaborate on projects, and expand your network of musicians, producers, and industry professionals.
  • Exposure: Showcase your music to a global audience. Gain exposure, attract fans, and unlock opportunities for recognition and collaboration.
